re-pair

Move through a crowd of of strangers and find your pair. You only have 20 seconds.

re-pair is a top-down game about finding your pair in a crowd of similar faces, with only a short amount of time to do so. The game forces the player to think quick and be watchful as the whole crowd moves, weaving between eachother.

The game was made using SFML in C++, with art assets, music and sound all produced by the team on various platforms. the game was designed and developed by a small team of three over a weekend for Global Game Jam 2020.

The game uses only images and gameplay to convey meaning, allowing the game to be played and understood by people of any language. Additionally, the game has a "blind mode" which uses sound to represent location, allowing even blind players to enjoy the game.
